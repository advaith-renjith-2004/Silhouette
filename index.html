Designed for Silence

A battery-saving, three-tier local verification sequence triggers only when needed.

🚶‍♂️

Tier 1: Motion Gate

Kinetix uses your phone's low-power activity sensors. Activity checks remain dormant until you register as completely stationary.

📱

Tier 2: Orientation Gate

Once stationary, the app checks if your screen is facing down on your desk. Gating checks prevent trigger actions when typing or using the device.

🎙️

Tier 3: Audio decibel check

A 1-second audio decibel scan verifies room quietness. The muting action is skipped in noisy meetings to ensure target accuracy.

🤝

Theme Cohesion

Personalize your experience with presets (Cyberpunk, Forest Oasis, Obsidian, Snow Drift). The entire interface, curves, and ambient animations shift to match.
